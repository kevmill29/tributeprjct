Michael Jackson

The King of Pop

Here is a timeline of Michael Jackson's life and accomplishments:

  • 1958 - Michael Jackson was born in Gary, Indiana.
  • 1964 - Michael Jackson joined the band "The Jackson Brothers"(later known as the Jackson 5) formed with his brothers Jackie, Tito and Jermaine. He was originally a background vocalist but later became a lead vocalist along with Jermaine.
  • 1968/1969 - The Jackson 5 is signed to Motown Records and Diana Ross introduces them to the public eye performing at the 1969 Miss Black America pageant.
  • 1970 - "I Want You Back" became the first Jackson 5 song to reach number on the Billboard hot 100. Three more singles "ABC","The Love You Save","I'll Be There" topped the charts as well.
  • 1979 - Michael released album "Off The Wall" which established him as a solo performer and reached number 3 on the billboard 200 chart. This album won many awards and won his first Grammy as a solo performer.
  • 1982 - Michael Jackson released the album "Thriller" which became the greatest selling album worldwide in 1983 and also the greatest selling album of all-time worldwide selling over 70 million copies. This also lead to him tying the record for most Grammy awards won in a single cermony with the band Santana at 8 awards.
  • 1987 - Five years later Jackson released "Bad", with the music industry expecting major success. This album sold close to 45 million copies world wide.
